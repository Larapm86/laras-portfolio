Restaurant Nevel

Brand & Strategy 2023 – 2024

Every once in a while, a project comes along that feels bigger than just work – it feels personal. Designing the visual identity for my friends at Nevel was exactly that kind of project. Our bond runs deep and our shared history became the inspiration for this work.

Concept

Nestled in the city center of ’s-Hertogenbosch, Restaurant Nevel operates with a profound connection to nature. Every dish they create celebrates local ingredients, sourced from nearby entrepreneurs and prepared with care, artistry, and respect.

The logo is rooted in the idea of growth. A blooming flower — a symbol of the restaurant at its peak. But no flower blooms without a planted seed. This journey, from seed to full bloom, mirrors the growth of the restaurant’s owners.

To capture this story, we developed a short yet meaningful animation, bringing their journey to life.

Caption for second image.

Restaurant Nevel has blossomed into something extraordinary. With its innovative concept and thoughtful execution, it has sparked a social buzz that exceeded all expectations. From seed to blossom.
